How It Works
1. Copy the link above and distribute it via email, your HR system, or a company bulletin.
2. Participants open the link and enter their name, email, and optional details (employee ID, department, date of birth).
3. Registered participants appear in the Participants tab and in the screener portal lookup.
4. Alternatively, upload a CSV of participants directly in the Participants tab.
